Welcome to the Steve Wozniak's Fan Page

Welcome to my site dedicated to an all time legend and one of the greatest inventors of our time, Steve Wozniak. He is the man who co-founded Apple Computer, which is now known as Apple Inc. with Steve Jobs and Ronald Wayne. You can say that Steve Wozniak is the mastermind who single handedly designed both the Apple I and Apple II computers in the late 1970's which pushed forward to the development of microcomputer revolution.
