About Steve Wozniak

Although Steve Wozniak left his full-time employment with Apple on February 6, 1987. Wozniak still remains as an employee of Apple spiritually. Now being a teacher and a Philanthropist, he provides students and schools with computers and financial packages that promote them for their innovative use of computers in the fields of business, art and music.
